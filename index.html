Welcome to OS in Browser

Introduction

Welcome to our interactive platform where you can explore the design and functionality of different operating systems—right in your browser! This project is a web-based educational tool that simulates the unique user experiences and interfaces of popular operating systems, allowing users to compare and contrast their designs interactively.

Our goal is to bring the look and feel of Windows, macOS, and Ubuntu directly to your browser, giving you insights into the design choices and user experiences of each OS.

Project Overview

Our platform covers the following operating systems:

  • Windows: Dive into the clean and structured interface of Windows, known for its user-friendly navigation and productivity features.
  • macOS: Experience the sleek and polished design of macOS, celebrated for its seamless user experience and aesthetic appeal.
  • Ubuntu: Discover the minimalist and open-source design of Ubuntu, recognized for its flexibility and customization options.

Through this project, we aim to replicate the core elements of these operating systems using modern web technologies like HTML and CSS. Whether you’re here to learn, compare, or simply explore, our platform provides an educational journey into the world of OS design.
