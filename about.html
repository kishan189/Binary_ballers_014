About OS World

Welcome to OS World, a web-based educational platform where you can dive deep into the design and functionality of the three most popular operating systems: Windows, macOS, and Ubuntu. This project aims to provide an immersive learning experience, allowing users to explore and compare the unique elements that define each OS.

Our Mission

Our goal is to bridge the gap between design and user experience across different operating systems by providing an interactive and comprehensive platform for learning and exploration.
